Age 20
Course INFORMATION MANAGEMENT


Hi! I’m someone who feels most at home in the pages of a mystery novel, especially the kind that slowly pulls you into the shadows and refuses to let go until the very end. I have a soft spot for horror and mystery stories, where every detail matters and every silence means something. When I’m not reading, you’ll usually find me listening to music, with “To You” by SEVENTEEN playing on repeat—it’s my comfort song, the kind that feels like a quiet conversation with my thoughts.


My favorite color is black, not because it’s dark, but because it’s calm, classic, and full of depth—just like the stories I love. Food-wise, nothing beats nasi goreng tomyum for me; it’s spicy, comforting, and always hits the right spot. I also love unwinding by watching sitcoms and crime shows, especially Friends, Brooklyn 99, and Criminal Minds—they’re my go-to for both laughter and clever storytelling.


You might notice there’s no picture of me here, and that’s intentional. I like the idea of being a little mysterious—after all, some things don’t need to be seen to be understood. I believe the best way to know someone is through their words, their interests, and the stories they choose to love ✨
